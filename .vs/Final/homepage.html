About

Account Aggregator is an entity that facilitates structured financial data sharing from FIP to FIU with the consent of the user.User can access and mange their financial data.Additionally you will be able to choose the period for which the data can be shared with FIU.


The Aggregator stores the user's information in the database which ensures easy data management.They can also edit their information.It ensures data security through encryption.


It reduces paper works and saves time.It also reduces the cost of transcation.The users can view their transactions,savings and balance.

It builds trust to both users and the bank.It establishes data security and data management.Nobody gets access to your data without your consent.
